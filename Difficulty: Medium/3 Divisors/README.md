<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">queries[] = [6]

<strong>Explanation: </strong>There is only one number 4 which has exactly three divisors 1, 2 and 4 and less than equal to 6.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>query[] = [3, 10]
<strong>Output: </strong>[0, 2]
<strong>Explanation: </strong>For query[0], the answer is 0. For query[1], there are two numbers 4 and 9 having exactly 3 divisors.</span></pre>
